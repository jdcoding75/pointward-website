Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Pointward is built on a simple principle: the least data possible, used only to connect you with the people you love.

What we collect

When you use Pointward with connecting features, we collect:

Account information — When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier from Apple. We never receive your real email address unless you choose to share it.

Location data — Your device location is used to calculate the direction and distance to your saved places. Location is processed on your device and never stored on our servers.

Thoughts sent — When you send a thought to a connected person, the emoji and timestamp are stored temporarily to deliver it. Once delivered and felt, thoughts are kept in history for your reference only.

Device token — To deliver push notifications we store your device's push token. This is used only to deliver thoughts from people you've chosen to connect with.

What we don't collect

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, photos, contacts, or any personal information beyond what is listed above.

We do not sell your data. Ever.

We do not use your data for advertising.

We do not share your data with third parties except as required to operate the service (Apple push notifications, Supabase database hosting).

Offline use

Pointward works completely offline for the core compass experience. No data is collected or transmitted when using the app without connecting to others.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on servers in the United States. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account.

You can use Pointward offline without creating an account — no data is collected in this mode.

Children

Pointward is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

If we make significant changes to this policy we will notify you through the app.

Contact

Questions about privacy? hello@pointward.app
